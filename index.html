About This Platform

InvestMind is a behavioral profiling platform built on a machine learning pipeline developed from primary survey research on investor behavior. The underlying analysis uses questionnaire data measuring four key constructs that influence investment decision-making.

The ML pipeline employs Gradient Boosting Classification with exhaustive hyperparameter tuning (GridSearchCV), SHAP-based explainability, and cross-dataset validation using Kolmogorov-Smirnov tests.

Behavioral Constructs

  • Market Information (MI1–MI3): Reliance on news, analyst reports, and financial media
  • Herding Behavior (HB1–HB4): Following crowd behavior in buy/sell decisions
  • Heuristics (He1–He4): Cognitive shortcuts — anchoring, availability, representativeness, overconfidence
  • Stock Knowledge (SK1–SK3): Financial literacy and understanding of market mechanics
  • Investment Attitude (AT1–AT6): Target variable — overall investment decision propensity

Classification Logic

Investment Decision Class is determined by the composite AT_score (average of AT1–AT6):

  • Low: AT_score ≤ 3.0 — Low investment decision propensity
  • Moderate: AT_score 3.0 – 4.2 — Moderate propensity, responsive to context
  • High: AT_score > 4.2 — Strong investment decision drive
